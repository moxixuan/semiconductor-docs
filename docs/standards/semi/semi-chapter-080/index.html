  17. 1 Batch Blending  The proces s of chemical blending where the chemical constituents are combined and mixed in a tank before being made available for use.

  19. 3 Feedback Controlled Blending  Blending process which combines constituents based on measurements of the blended chemical. At a sample flow rate of 0. 05 scfm, the time required is one hour. Ensure that the background counts are stable or decreasing.
