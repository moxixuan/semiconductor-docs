A sample of the solution prepared in Section 8.1.4 is titrated with standard iodine solution to a starch end- point (blue).   14. 4 Silicon Tetrafluoride — This procedure describes the determination of silicon tetrafluoride in boron trifluoride gas. This method is based on the reaction of soluble silica with molybdate ion to form a greenish- yellow complex, B-silicomolybdic acid.
